<h2 class="wp-block-heading">🚀 The Rise of Sora and Runway ML: An Overview</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"className":""} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🔹 What is Sora?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p><strong>Sora</strong> is an AI-based video platform that simplifies editing using machine learning. It offers automated features like background removal, cinematic filters, and template-driven editing—<em>no pro skills required</em>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"className":""} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🔹 What is Runway ML?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p><strong>Runway ML</strong> is a creative AI toolkit with real-time editing, text-to-video, and a wide range of generative models. It blends seamlessly into your workflow with integrations for Premiere Pro, Figma, and more.</p>

<p><strong>Result</strong>: 3x engagement boost and 70% reduction in video production costs.</p>
