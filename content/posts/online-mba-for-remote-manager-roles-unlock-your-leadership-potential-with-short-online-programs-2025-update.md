<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Introduction: Remote Management Is Here to Stay—and MBA Programs Are Adapting</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Remote work continues its steady rise as of 2025. This trend is creating demand for leaders skilled in digital collaboration. There is also a need for expertise in virtual team dynamics. Across industries—from tech to consulting—organizations are seeking managers who can empower distributed teams effectively.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>To meet this need, top business schools are rapidly evolving online MBA offerings. These programs now include specialized tracks in digital leadership, sustainability, and AI. They also offer tracks in remote team management. These tracks prepare candidates for tomorrow’s challenges.</p>
