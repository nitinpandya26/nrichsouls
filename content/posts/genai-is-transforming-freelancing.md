<h2 class="wp-block-heading">🔍 How GenAI is Transforming Freelancing: Key Use Cases</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"className":""} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. ✍️ Content Creation and Copywriting</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<p><strong>Tool:</strong> ChatGPT, Jasper.ai<br><strong>Outcome:</strong> Quick blog posts, website content, captions, ad copy<br><strong>Real-Life Example:</strong><br>A freelance copywriter automates first drafts with ChatGPT. They use their saved time to take on more clients. This approach doubles their income in 3 months.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"className":""} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. 🎨 Design and Visual Assets</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p><strong>Tool:</strong> DALL·E, Midjourney, Canva AI<br><strong>Outcome:</strong> Logos, social media creatives, product mockups<br><strong>Real-Life Example:</strong><br>An illustrator uses Midjourney for initial sketches. <h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. 💻 Coding and App Development</h3>
